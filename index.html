Why STARS?

Many students struggle with mental health issues either due to academics, or extracurriculars as well as other social aspects of school. To combat this, we created STARS, a mobile application that uses machine learning and artificial intelligence to analyze user data to suggest daily mental health activities as well as effective study sessions based on schedules for college students. STARS also has the ability to connect users with others on the app by sharing completed activities to encourage each other.
